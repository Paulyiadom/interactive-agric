Welcome to Agrisufficiency Gh

Introduction Background

This innovative product is centered on SDG 2 which aims to end hunger, achieve food security, improved nutrition, and sustainable agriculture.

Problem Statement

Ghana's hunger index has improved over the years from 28.5 in 1998 to 13.9 at present, representing a moderate condition (International Food Policy Research Institute, IFPRI, 2024).

Product / Solution

The product'Agrisufficiency Gh'seeks to model statiscally sustainable food security in Agriculture using data extensively from the STATSBANK

Estimates & Forecast

  • Self Sufficiency Ratio(SSR)
  • Food Self Sufficiency Ratio(FSSR)
  • Affordability index (AI)
  • Food Stock Reserve Ratio(FSRR)
  • Nutritional Adequacy Ratio (NAR)
